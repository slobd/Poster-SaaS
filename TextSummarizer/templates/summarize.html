PosterLab Summarizer

Notes for Usage:
You can either write your own text or upload a pdf with a text to let the system summarize it. Please note that your text or pdf should at least contain one correct sentence with punctuation, and enough expressive words. If the system can not detect your text, it will not proceed.

The summarizer will take the 3 most important statements and put them into a short summary

Allow for a couple seconds to pass for the summarization results to display
